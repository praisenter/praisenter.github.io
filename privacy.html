PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: 2026-01-21

Overview

Praisenter is designed with privacy as a core principle. The Praisenter application operates primarily on the local system and does not collect, track, transmit, or store personal data outside of the user's device.

Any limited usage information that may be collected is handled by the application distribution platforms (such as operating system app stores), not by Praisenter itself.

Information Collection and Use

Praisenter does not directly collect or process personal information.

  • No personal data is collected by the application
  • No in-application usage tracking or telemetry is performed
  • No application data is transmitted to external servers
  • No analytics, advertising, or marketing frameworks are used
  • No cloud services or remote APIs are accessed

All application content and configuration data remain entirely on the local system under the user's control.

App Store Usage

When Praisenter is installed or used, limited usage information may be collected by the application store or operating system platform through which the app is distributed (for example, the Microsoft Store, Apple App Store,Ubuntu Snap Store, or similar services).

This information may include:

  • Installation counts
  • Crash reports
  • Basic usage or performance metrics

This data is collected and processed by the app store provider, not by Praisenter, and is subject to the privacy policy of the respective platform. Praisenter does not receive, store, or control any personally identifiable information from these sources.

Screen Capture / Recording

Praisenter includes a few features that use screen capture/recording. Screen capture/recording is used solely for visual aids or the presentation of content within the application.

  • Screen capture is performed locally on the user's system
  • Captured visuals are not transmitted or uploaded by Praisenter
  • Screen capture is initiated and controlled by the user
  • Captured content remains accessible only to the user unless shared externally by the user

Praisenter does not monitor, analyze, or retain screen content beyond its intended local use.

Data Storage

All Praisenter data, including presentations, media, settings, and any screen-captured visuals, are stored locally on the user's device.

  • No cloud uploads
  • No cross-device synchronization
  • No remote backups

Users are responsible for managing their own local data and backups.

Third-Party Services

Aside from distribution via app stores, Praisenter does not integrate with third-party services for analytics, advertising, or data collection.

Website

This website uses Google Analytics to track usage of the website itself. The information is aggregate in nature, consisting of metrics like browser, operating system, country/region, screen size, and so on.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect application changes, platform requirements, or legal obligations. Updates will be made available through the Praisenter website or application distribution channels.

If you have any questions about how information is tracked, retained, or used, please contact us through a discussion.
